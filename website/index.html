About Yukthi

Welcome to Yukthi TechFest, explore the tech in you!
Event brought by the Department of Computer Science and Applications of St Mary's College (AUTONOMOUS), Thrissur. This is a creative and tech hub to gather brilliant minds, cutting-edge ideas, and groundbreaking solutions under one roof. Yukthi is a joyous community excited to welcome aspiring coders, innovators, and teen techies to celebrate creativity and discovery. Yukthi celebrates the spirit of technology and innovation and ignites the spark of one's hidden talent by giving them an open platform to showcase and explore it. This platform helps you in taking your skills, ideas, and abilities to the next level. Yukthi aspires to bring the best tech wizards of our country to shape a better tomorrow. So join us in this great festival of learning and sharing of ideas as Yukthi awaits for you, to help in exploring the tech in you!
